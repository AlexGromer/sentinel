# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality,
personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment: empathy and kindness, respect for
differing opinions, graceful acceptance of constructive feedback, focusing on what is best for the
community. Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, trolling, insults, publishing others' private
information, and other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project maintainer via the
repository owner's profile contact. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and
fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

Full text and enforcement guidelines: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.
